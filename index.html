Game Basics

Controls

Learn the intuitive touch controls: left joystick to move, right side to aim and attack. Master the auto-aim feature for quick reactions.

Progression

Earn trophies to unlock new brawlers and progress through leagues. Collect power points and coins to upgrade your brawlers.

Currency

Understand coins, gems, star points, and club coins. Learn the best ways to spend each currency for maximum efficiency.

Super & Gadgets

Each brawler has a unique Super ability that charges by dealing damage. Learn when and how to use these powerful abilities.

Gadgets are special abilities that can be used 3 times per match. These can turn the tide of battle when used strategically.

Pro Tips & Strategies

Advanced techniques to help you dominate the battlefield

General Tips

  • Always keep moving - standing still makes you an easy target
  • Learn to manual aim - auto-aim isn't always the best option
  • Watch the minimap to track enemy positions and avoid surprises
  • Save your Super for crucial moments rather than using it immediately

Advanced Strategies

  • Learn spawn locations in Showdown to control power cube boxes
  • In Gem Grab, designate roles: gem carrier, support, and aggressor
  • In Brawl Ball, passing is often better than trying to dribble through enemies
  • Time your attacks to hit enemies as they exit spawn invincibility
